Project Overview:

  This project involves an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution to analyze Car Sales Performance. 
The goal was to transform raw automotive sales data into an interactive dashboard that 
identifies key revenue drivers, customer demographics, and market trends to support data-driven decision-making 
for a dealership or manufacturer.

🎯 Business Objectives:

  Revenue Tracking: Monitor the $5M total revenue milestone.

  Customer Profiling: Analyze the relationship between Annual Income and purchasing power.

  Market Segmentation: Understand sales distribution by Gender and Manufacturer.

  Trend Analysis: Identify growth patterns across fiscal quarters (Q1–Q4).

🛠️ Technical Stack:

  Power BI Desktop: Report authoring and UI/UX design.

  Power Query (M): Data cleaning, type conversion, and ETL processing.

  DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Created measures for dynamic KPIs and aggregations.

Microsoft Excel: Original data source.

📊 Dashboard Features & Insights:

  KPI Header: Displays $5M Total Revenue using a card visual with custom icons.

  Income Analysis: A clustered bar chart showing Sum of Annual Income by Customer, highlighting the highest-value segments.

  Gender Demographics: A Donut Chart revealing that Male customers contribute $4.07M (79.61%) of total sales.

  Growth Trend: A Line Chart visualizing a steady increase in sales price from Q1 through Q4.

Brand Performance: A Treemap categorized by Company, showing the dominance of brands like Chevrolet, Ford, and Toyota.

Interactive Slicers: Custom buttons for brand-specific filtering (e.g., Hyundai, BMW, Audi).

🚀 Key Takeaways:

  Targeting: The data suggests a heavy lean toward high-income male demographics, representing a primary marketing target.

  Seasonality: Sales show a strong upward trajectory toward the end of the year (Q4), suggesting seasonal demand or year-end promotions.

  Brand Variety: The market is highly fragmented across many manufacturers, with no single brand holding a monopoly
